about me

 
 

Hi! I’m Ashley.

I’m currently a junior at UCLA, studying Cognitive Science with a minor in Digital Humanities.

Most recently, I interned at Live Nation Entertainment and worked within the Operations department. Before this, I worked at an investment research firm and a pet snacks startup.

At UCLA, I like to keep myself busy through my involvement in campus organizations. Some of my favorite clubs include cultural orgs dedicated to advocacy for the Vietnamese community and business orgs focused on the professional development of students.
